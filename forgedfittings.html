Forged Fittings

Forged pipe fittings are made according toASME B16.11, MSS-SP-79\83\95\97 and BS3799 standards. It is a connector in between nominal bore pipes. The fittings are made of carbon steel A105 and stainless steel SS316 material. The size available is from 1/8” to 4” Forged pipe fittings was named via its manufacturing forging process, which was to heat alloy steel or carbon in a transforming temperature and moulds the heated parts into their customizable shape.Forged pipe fitting designed as pressure class 2000, 3000, 6000 and 9000.
